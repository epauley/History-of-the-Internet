Introduction

How many times have you used the Internet today? Once, twice, too many times to count? For most people it is the latter. Today, the Internet has become an essential part of our everyday lives by keeping us connected with those around us, sharing knowledge, and even supporting our dreams. What started off as two computers sending a message between each other has evolved into a world-wide connection but most people take for granted its humble start.
